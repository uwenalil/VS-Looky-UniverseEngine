Changes the Scrolltype -
 Value 1 or 2:
   'off' is to switch to Upscroll(v1) or Sidescroll(v2)
   'on' is to switch to Downscroll(v1) or Middlescroll(v2)
   'swap' is to switch between Scrolltypes
   'default' is to switch to the Default Scrolltype
   'anti' is to switch to Opposite of the Default Scrolltype

Use "ChaSrlTyp~ Tween Typing" to do 'insta' instead!
